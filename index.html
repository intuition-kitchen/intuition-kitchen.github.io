LIVE MUSIC
KIZ CARTER, JUKE JACKSON & WAVE MILOR
Lisa “Kiz” Carter has been playing the guitar and singing though out Virginia at festivals, breweries, wineries and clubs for over 3 decades. Lisa has a CD of original songs, “Out of the Blues”, released in 2010 and a second CD with Jack “Juke Jackson” Roy (“Hanging on the Line”) that was released in 2015.

Together, Kiz and Juke play original songs and cover many of the blues greats like Ma Rainy, Koko Taylor, R.L. Burnside and Howlin’ Wolf. Wave Milor brings his talent on harmonica and vocals to the group. He is an active member of multiple bands in Virginia and Maryland including Hard Swimmin’ Fish and $12 Bacon.
