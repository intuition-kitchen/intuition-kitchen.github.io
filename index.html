LIVE MUSIC
BRENT HULL
Brent Andrew Hull is an Augusta County native that has been performing regionally for the past 25 plus years either solo, duo or in the full band, "Maybe Tomorrow".

Hull plays a wide variety including: country, pop, rock, hip-hop, and original songs and no two shows are the same. He is famous for his “mash ups” that manage to mix songs that you would not think went together.

Hull has shared the stage with musical greats such as, Dave Matthews, and Jimmy Fortune. He is also an accomplished actor, and has acted/sang on many local stages including Oak Grove, ShenanArts, Entree Act and Mary Baldwin. Feel free to request songs!
